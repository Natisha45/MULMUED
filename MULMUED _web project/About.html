About MULUMED Trading PLC

MULUMED Trading PLC is dedicated to strengthening Ethiopia’s healthcare system by supplying high-quality pharmaceutical products and medical solutions to pharmacies, clinics, and hospitals.

Who We Are

MULUMED Trading PLC is a pharmaceutical import and distribution company committed to providing safe, reliable, and high-quality healthcare products across Ethiopia.

We collaborate with trusted international manufacturers to supply essential medicines, diagnostic equipment, and hospital consumables that meet global quality standards.

Vision

To become a trusted pharmaceutical partner improving healthcare accessibility across Ethiopia and the African region.

Mission

To provide high-quality pharmaceutical products and healthcare solutions that empower individuals to live healthier lives.

Values

Integrity • Quality • Reliability • Innovation

What We Do

Pharmaceutical Import

Importing certified pharmaceutical products from trusted international manufacturers.

Medical Equipment Supply

Supplying laboratory and diagnostic equipment for healthcare facilities.

Hospital Consumables

Providing everyday medical supplies including gloves, syringes, IV fluids, and disinfectants.
